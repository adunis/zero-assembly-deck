# ZERO ASSEMBLY - KEYWORD REFERENCE

Quick reference guide for all equipment keywords. Keep this next to your battlefield!

---

## WEAPON TYPE KEYWORDS

### **Direct Fire**
Requires line of sight. Affected by cover modifiers. Roll 2D6 to hit (need 7+).

### **Indirect Fire**
No line of sight needed. Ignores all cover. -1 to hit penalty.

### **Lock-On**
Two-turn process:
- **Turn 1:** LOCK action (1 AP) - Roll 2D6, need 7+, requires LoS, must be >15cm away
- **Turn 2:** FIRE action (1 AP) - Auto-hit, no LoS needed, ignores cover
- Lock breaks if: target moves within 15cm, shooter moves, countermeasures deployed

### **Melee**
Requires base contact with target. Auto-hit (no roll needed). Requires LoS.

---

## RANGE KEYWORDS

### **Short** (0-15cm)
Close range. +1 to hit modifier.

### **Medium** (0-30cm)
Standard engagement range. No modifier.

### **Long** (0-45cm)
Extended range. -1 to hit modifier.

---

## DAMAGE KEYWORDS

### **X DP** (Damage Points)
Amount of damage dealt on hit. Place X HP tokens at target's feet.
- **1 DP** = Light damage
- **2 DP** = Medium damage
- **3 DP** = Heavy damage
- **0 DP** = No damage (utility effect only)

---

## COMBAT EFFECT KEYWORDS

### **Multi-Shot X**
Roll 2D6 to hit X times. Each successful hit deals full weapon damage. Each hit triggers 1 Finger Flick.

**Example:** Multi-Shot 2 = Roll 2D6 twice. If both hit, deal 2× damage and perform 2 Finger Flicks.

### **Heavy**
Perform 2 Finger Flicks instead of 1 when you hit. Represents massive kinetic impact.

### **Knockback X**
Perform X Finger Flicks on hit, even if weapon deals 0 DP. Pure knockback effect.

### **Piercing**
Ignores Partial Cover (-1 penalty). Still affected by Heavy Cover (-2 penalty).

### **Hazard X**
On hit, place X hazard markers on the battlefield. Enemy mechs touching hazard markers take 1 DP automatically.

### **Reliable**
Re-roll one missed attack per turn. Represents consistent, dependable weapons.

---

## DEFENSE KEYWORDS

### **Shield X SP** (Shield Points)
Energy shield with X capacity. Absorbs hits:
- Lose 1 SP per hit (regardless of weapon's DP)
- No HP damage while shield is active
- **Blocks Finger Flicks** while active
- When shield reaches 0 SP, it's depleted (further hits damage HP normally)

### **Recharge**
Restore shield capacity. 1 AP = 1 SP (up to shield's maximum).
- **Cannot RECHARGE and ENGAGE in the same turn**
- Choose offense or defense each turn

### **Reactive Armor**
When hit by a 3 DP weapon, reduce damage to 2 DP. Does not affect 1 DP or 2 DP weapons.

### **Heavy Armor**
Grants bonus HP but reduces movement by 5cm.

---

## UTILITY KEYWORDS

### **Free SCAVENGE**
SCAVENGE action costs 0 AP instead of 1 AP. Can only be used once per turn.

### **Damage +1**
Your next attack gains +1 DP. Single use per activation.

### **Fast Move**
Once per turn, one MOVE action covers 2 rulers instead of 1 (30cm for legs, 60cm for wheels/treads).

### **Jump Jets**
Climb up to 10cm high terrain (instead of standard 5cm limit).

### **Lock-On Assist**
When attempting to LOCK with Lock-On weapons, get +1 to the lock roll. Does not help with firing.

---

## COUNTERMEASURE KEYWORDS

### **Countermeasure**
Equipment that breaks Lock-On targeting. See specific type below.

### **Reactive**
Triggers automatically when enemy tries to LOCK on you. No AP cost. Roll to break lock.

**Example:** Chaff Flares - When enemy tries to LOCK, roll 1D6. On 4+, lock fails.

### **Active (1 AP)**
Spend 1 AP to activate effect. Breaks existing locks or creates defensive effects.

**Example:** Smoke ECM Pod - Spend 1 AP to break all locks on you.

### **Limited X**
Can only be used X times per game. Track uses with tokens.

### **Unlimited**
No usage limit. Can be used every turn.

---

## KEYWORD COMBINATIONS

Equipment can combine multiple keywords for unique effects:

### Assault Rifle
**Direct Fire** • **Medium** (0-30cm) • **1 DP**
- Standard weapon: needs LoS, affected by cover, medium range

### Heavy Missile Launcher
**Lock-On** • **Long** (0-45cm) • **3 DP** • **Heavy**
- Two-turn lock, auto-hit, long range, 2 Finger Flicks

### Carpet Bomber
**Indirect Fire** • **Long** (0-45cm) • **1 DP** • **Multi-Shot 4**
- No LoS needed, ignores cover, 4 separate attacks

### Sonic Repulsor
**Direct Fire** • **Short** (0-15cm) • **0 DP** • **Knockback 3**
- No damage, but 3 Finger Flicks for pure knockback

### Energy Shield
**Shield 3 SP** • **Recharge**
- Absorbs 3 hits, blocks Finger Flicks, 1 AP = 1 SP

### Stealth System
**Countermeasure** • **Unlimited**
- Passive -2 to enemy lock rolls, always active

## GAMEPLAY TIPS

### Reading Equipment Cards
1. **Scan keywords first** (bold text) - Tells you what the equipment does
2. **Check reminder text** (italic text) - Explains specific mechanics
3. **Look at stat line** (bottom) - Quick reference for damage/range

### Building Your Mech
- **Direct Fire** weapons are reliable but need LoS
- **Indirect Fire** bypasses cover but has -1 to hit
- **Lock-On** guarantees hits but takes 2 turns
- **Shields** block Finger Flicks (great vs Heavy weapons)
- **Countermeasures** are essential vs Lock-On enemies

### Combat Strategy
- **Multi-Shot** weapons are great for chip damage
- **Heavy** weapons excel at knocking over enemies
- **Piercing** weapons counter light cover
- **Knockback** weapons control positioning
- **Hazard** weapons create area denial
