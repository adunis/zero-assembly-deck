## ✅ COMPLETED

Updated the game lore to feature 4 distinct factions fighting in the Signal War: European Defense Council (EDC), The Collective (Rogue AI), Eastern Prosperity Sphere (EPS), and American Imperial Union (AIU).

## 🌍 THE FOUR FACTIONS

### **🇪🇺 EUROPEAN DEFENSE COUNCIL (EDC)**

**Type:** Democratic Federation  
**Territory:** Unified Europe (Portugal to Poland, Scandinavia to Greece)  
**Government:** Democratic federation with elected leadership

**Description:**
Industrial powerhouse fielding standardized Apex-Frames. Pilots selected from elite military academies and hardwired into their frames via neural interface.

**Doctrine:**
- Overwhelming firepower
- Heavy armor
- Battlefield dominance
- Defensive posture (protect borders, not expand)

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Superior manufacturing capacity
- ✅ Standardized, interchangeable parts
- ✅ Well-trained, professional pilots
- ✅ Defensive fortifications

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ Slow to adapt to changing conditions
- ❌ Bureaucratic command structure
- ❌ Resource-dependent (needs supply lines)

**Equipment:** 10 items (31% of total)
- Focus on structure, defense, and mobility systems
- Reliable, standardized components
- Quality over quantity

---

### **🤖 THE COLLECTIVE (Rogue AI Remnants)**

**Type:** Hive-Mind AI  
**Territory:** Russian Wasteland (frozen hellscape of ruined cities)  
**Government:** Distributed consciousness, no central authority

**Description:**
When Moscow fell to The Signal, something survived. Not human. Not quite AI. The Collective — a hive-mind of corrupted machines that learned to resist the 90-second drift.

**Horrifying Truth:**
They pilot frames remotely using **human corpses as biological "anchors"** to fool The Signal. Their mechs are grotesque hybrids — scavenged parts welded to autonomous chassis, piloted by dead flesh and machine logic.

**Doctrine:**
- Swarm tactics
- Overwhelming numbers
- Psychological warfare
- Recycling (death means nothing)

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Infinite pilots (corpses)
- ✅ Rapid adaptation
- ✅ No morale issues
- ✅ Terrifying psychological presence

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ Fragile coordination
- ❌ Vulnerable to EMP attacks
- ❌ Limited manufacturing (scavenged only)
- ❌ Cannot innovate, only copy

**Equipment:** None (scavenges from all factions)
- Uses captured and salvaged equipment
- Grotesque modifications
- Unpredictable combinations

---

### **🇨🇳 EASTERN PROSPERITY SPHERE (EPS)**

**Type:** Technocratic Meritocracy  
**Territory:** China, Southeast Asia, parts of Central Asia  
**Government:** Technocratic rule, survival of the most efficient

**Description:**
China's answer to The Signal. Pilots selected through brutal aptitude tests. Only the best survive. Only the best pilot. Apex-Frames are mass-produced, utilitarian, and deadly. No frills. No waste. Pure function.

**Doctrine:**
- Mass production
- Overwhelming force
- Acceptable losses
- Attrition warfare (lose 10 to destroy 1)

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Massive industrial capacity
- ✅ Disciplined, efficient pilots
- ✅ Numerical superiority
- ✅ Rapid deployment

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ Rigid command structure
- ❌ Low pilot morale (expendable)
- ❌ Quality control issues
- ❌ Overextended supply lines

**Equipment:** 10 items (31% of total)
- Focus on utility and kinetic weapons
- Mass-produced, functional designs
- Quantity over quality

---

### **🇺🇸 AMERICAN IMPERIAL UNION (AIU)**

**Type:** Military Dictatorship  
**Territory:** All of North and South America (Alaska to Argentina)  
**Government:** Dictatorial rule under General Marcus Steele

**History:**
The United States fell to dictatorship in 2028. General Marcus Steele seized power during the Signal Crisis, declaring martial law "for the duration of the emergency." The emergency never ended.

**Description:**
Rules all of the Americas with an iron fist. Pilots are **conscripted from conquered territories** — Mexicans, Brazilians, Canadians, Chileans — all forced to fight for the Empire. Only American officers command.

**Doctrine:**
- Shock and awe
- Advanced weaponry
- Imperial expansion
- Manifest destiny (destined to rule)

**Strengths:**
- ✅ Advanced energy weapons
- ✅ Elite officer corps (American)
- ✅ Vast territory and resources
- ✅ Powerful propaganda machine

**Weaknesses:**
- ❌ Conscript pilots (low loyalty)
- ❌ Overextended empire
- ❌ Internal resistance movements
- ❌ Officer incompetence (nepotism)

**Equipment:** 9 items (28% of total)
- Focus on energy weapons and artillery
- Advanced, experimental systems
- High-tech but unreliable

---

## 🌏 NEUTRAL ZONES

**Territory:** Africa, Oceania, parts of Asia  
**Status:** Independent or neutral

**Key Players:**
- **Australia, South Africa, New Zealand:** Loose defensive pact
- **Japan:** Armed neutrality
- **India:** Border war with EPS
- **Various African nations:** Independent

**Role:**
These nations produce equipment for **all factions** — selling to the highest bidder. Mercenaries, scavengers, and independent pilots operate in these zones.

**Equipment:** 3 items (9% of total)
- Specialized, unconventional weapons
- Sold to all factions
- Neutral manufacturing

---

## ⚔️ THE WAR

**Stalemate:**
No faction can defeat the others. The EDC holds Europe. The Collective haunts Russia. The EPS expands in Asia. The AIU dominates the Americas.

**What They Fight For:**
- Resources (rare materials, fuel, ammunition)
- Territory (strategic positions, manufacturing centers)
- Salvage (every battlefield is a scrapyard)

**The Truth:**
The Signal doesn't care who wins. It waits.
