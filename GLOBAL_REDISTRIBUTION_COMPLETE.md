## 🗺️ LORE IMPLICATIONS

### **The Signal War Goes Global**

The redistribution reflects the global nature of the Signal War. While the original conflict started in Europe, the Signal corruption has spread worldwide, forcing all nations to adopt the same solution: human-piloted, modular combat frames.

**Regional Characteristics:**

**🇪🇺 Europe (EU):**
- Industrial powerhouse
- Standardized, modular components
- Focus on structure, defense, and mobility
- Apex-Frame doctrine: heavy armor, reliable systems

**🌏 Asia:**
- Diverse manufacturing base
- Emphasis on utility and kinetic weapons
- Pragmatic, efficient designs
- Mix of high-tech (Japan, Korea) and mass-production (China, India)

**🌎 Americas:**
- Advanced energy weapons and artillery
- Focus on firepower and range
- North America: precision systems (USA, Canada)
- South America: area-effect weapons (Brazil, Argentina, Chile)

**🌍 Africa/Oceania:**
- Specialized, unconventional weapons
- Hazard systems and demolition tools
- Adaptive, resourceful designs
- Australia: heavy artillery, South Africa: area denial, New Zealand: close-range hazards
