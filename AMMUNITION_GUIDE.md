# Zero Assembly - Ammunition Guide

## 🎯 Device & Ammunition System

### 🏹 3D-PRINTED CROSSBOW (Direct-Fire)
**Trajectory:** Straight line - what you aim at is what you hit  
**Proxy Distance:** SHORT (1 Short Ruler)  
**Best For:** Precision shots, direct engagements, sniping

#### Ammunition Types:
1. **Standard Bolt** 🔫
   - Single projectile
   - Standard damage (1 DP)
   - Used by: Rifles, Railguns, Energy Beams

2. **Burst Bolt** 🔫🔫 or 🔫🔫🔫
   - Wider bolt representing multiple shots
   - 2-3 projectiles per shot
   - Used by: Twin-Link (x2), Tri-Barrel (x3)

#### Weapons Using Crossbow:
- 🇪🇸 Indra G-36 Micro-Rifle (Standard Bolt)
- 🇧🇪 FN Herstal FAL-X Autocannon (Standard Bolt)
- 🇫🇮 Sako Twin-Link Assault Rifle (Burst Bolt x2)
- 🇲🇹 BAE Systems Lancer Railgun (Standard Bolt)
- 🇭🇷 Thales Paralyzer EMP Beam (Standard Bolt)
- 🇸🇰 Nexter Tri-Barrel Spread Cannon (Burst Bolt x3)
- 🇱🇹 OTO Melara Phalanx Gatling Array (Standard Bolt)

### 💨 AIR CANNON (Light Arc-Fire)
**Trajectory:** Arc - can shoot over low cover  
**Proxy Distance:** SHORT (1 Short Ruler)  
**Best For:** Hitting enemies behind cover, special effects

#### Ammunition Types:
1. **Standard Shell** 💥
   - Basic projectile
   - Standard effects
   - Used by: Sonic weapons

2. **Explosive Shell** 💥💥
   - Larger projectile head
   - Special effects (knockback, disable, etc.)
   - Used by: Breacher Shotgun

#### Weapons Using Air Cannon:
- 🇮🇸 Dassault Sonic Repulsor (Standard Shell - knockback)
- 🇱🇺 Benelli M10 Breacher Shotgun (Explosive Shell - weapon disable)

---

### 💥 BIG AIR CANNON (Heavy Arc-Fire)
**Trajectory:** High arc - can shoot over medium cover  
**Proxy Distance:** SHORT (1 Short Ruler)  
**Best For:** Heavy bombardment, area denial

#### Ammunition Types:
1. **Standard BIG Shell** 💣
   - Large projectile
   - Heavy damage (2 DP)
   - Used by: Heavy Cannons

2. **Explosive BIG Shell** 💣💣
   - Extra-large projectile head
   - Maximum impact + special effects
   - Used by: Avalanche Cannon, ECM Blaster

#### Weapons Using Big Air Cannon:
- 🇧🇬 Rheinmetall MK-20 Rhino Heavy Cannon (Standard BIG Shell)
- 🇱🇻 Nammo Avalanche Cannon (Explosive BIG Shell - knockback)
- 🇨🇭 Oerlikon KBA Dual Autocannon (Standard BIG Shell - retry)
- 🇩🇰 Terma Barracuda ECM Blaster (Explosive BIG Shell - perk disable)

---

### 🚀 MORTAR (Extreme Arc-Fire)
**Trajectory:** Extreme high arc - shoots over buildings  
**Proxy Distance:** LONG (1 Long Ruler)  
**Best For:** Strategic bombardment, carpet bombing, indirect fire

#### Ammunition Types:
1. **Mini Rocket** 🚀
   - Small missiles
   - Precision strikes
   - 2-4 projectiles per shot
   - Used by: Micro-Missile Pod, Starstreak

2. **Standard Shell** 💥
   - Same as Air Cannon ammo
   - Carpet bombing
   - Up to 4 projectiles
   - Used by: Carpet-Bombing Pod

3. **Nuclear Bomb** ☢️
   - Giant ammunition
   - Must be dropped from above
   - Massive damage (2 DP) + terrain destruction
   - Used by: Demolition Pod

#### Weapons Using Mortar:
- 🇸🇮 Diehl Brimstone Micro-Missile Pod (Mini Rocket x2)
- 🇳🇱 Fokker Starstreak Missile Rack (Mini Rocket x3)
- 🇪🇪 Saab RBS-70 Carpet-Bombing Pod (Standard Shell x4)
- 🇵🇹 EDP Renováveis Storm Shadow Demolition Pod (Nuclear Bomb)

## 📐 Trajectory Comparison

```
CROSSBOW (Direct-Fire):
Mech ----→----→----→ Target
         (straight line)

AIR CANNON (Light Arc):
Mech ----↗---↘---- Target
      (low arc, over small cover)

BIG AIR CANNON (Heavy Arc):
Mech ----↗----↘---- Target
      (high arc, over medium cover)

MORTAR (Extreme Arc):
Mech ----↗------↘---- Target
      (extreme arc, over buildings)
```

## 🎮 Tactical Guide

### When to Use Direct-Fire (Crossbow):
✅ Clear line of sight to target  
✅ Need precision/accuracy  
✅ Target is in the open  
❌ Target is behind cover  
❌ Need to shoot over obstacles  

### When to Use Arc-Fire (Air Cannons):
✅ Target is behind low/medium cover  
✅ Need indirect fire  
✅ Want to hit multiple targets in area  
❌ Need precision shots  
❌ Target is in the open (direct-fire is better)  

### When to Use Strategic (Mortar):
✅ Target is behind tall cover/buildings  
✅ Need extreme indirect fire  
✅ Want to carpet bomb an area  
✅ Have elevated position  
❌ Need quick shots  
❌ Target is close range

## 🔧 Physical Setup

### Crossbow Setup:
1. Place crossbow 1 Short Ruler from your mech
2. Aim directly at target (straight line)
3. Load Standard Bolt or Burst Bolt
4. Fire - projectile travels straight

### Air Cannon Setup:
1. Place cannon 1 Short Ruler from your mech
2. Angle upward for arc trajectory
3. Load Standard Shell or Explosive Shell
4. Fire - projectile arcs over cover

### Big Air Cannon Setup:
1. Place cannon 1 Short Ruler from your mech
2. Angle upward for high arc
3. Load Standard BIG Shell or Explosive BIG Shell
4. Fire - projectile arcs high over cover

### Mortar Setup:
1. Place mortar 1 Long Ruler from your mech
2. Point upward for extreme arc
3. Load Mini Rockets, Standard Shells, or Nuclear Bomb
4. Fire - projectile(s) arc over buildings

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Crossbows are faster** - direct line means easier aiming
2. **Air Cannons are versatile** - can hit targets you can't see
3. **Mortars are strategic** - place them far away to shoot around corners
4. **Burst Bolts = Multiple Chances** - more projectiles = more chances to hit
5. **Explosive Shells = Special Effects** - bigger projectile heads mean special abilities
6. **Nuclear Bomb = Ultimate Weapon** - massive damage but requires careful positioning
