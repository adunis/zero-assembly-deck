# Rules Simplification - Complete

## 🎯 Goal
Remove unnecessary complexity while keeping the core physics-based gameplay intact.

---

## ✂️ What Was Removed

### 1. **Veteran Option** ❌
**Before:** Players with existing mechs could skip scavenging or choose between old/new  
**Why Remove:** Edge case that adds rules overhead for minimal benefit  
**Impact:** Cleaner Phase 2, everyone builds fresh each game

### 2. **Equipment Cubes** ❌
**Before:** Scavenge to grab random parts and modify mech mid-game  
**Why Remove:** Adds mid-game complexity, slows gameplay, hard to balance  
**Impact:** Faster games, cleaner SCAVENGE action  
**What Remains:** Resource Cubes (Neuralloy) for +1 HP healing

### 3. **Color Synergy System** ❌
**Before:** 1-3 colors = 1 Perk, 4+ colors = no Perk  
**Why Remove:** Pre-game complexity, limits creativity, hard to enforce  
**Impact:** Players choose 1 Perk before game starts (no restrictions)  
**Benefit:** More freedom in mech design, simpler rules

### 4. **AT (Armor Token) Markers** ❌
**Before:** Separate marker type for tracking Action Points  
**Why Remove:** Unnecessary physical component, players can track AP mentally  
**Impact:** One less marker type to print and manage  
**Alternative:** Use dice, coins, or just remember (you only have 2 AP)

### 5. **Board Edge Spacing Rule** ❌
**Before:** Terrain must be 15cm from board edges AND other terrain  
**Why Remove:** Unnecessary restriction, hard to enforce on different table sizes  
**Impact:** Simpler terrain placement, more flexible setup

---

## 📊 Simplification Impact

### Before Simplification:
- ❌ 6 marker types (HP, AT, Objectives, Resources, Equipment, Hazards)

## 💡 Design Philosophy

### What We Kept:
- ✅ **Physics-based combat** - Core gameplay loop
- ✅ **10-minute build phase** - Modular mech assembly
- ✅ **2 AP + free REPOSITION** - Action economy
- ✅ **Resource Cubes (Neuralloy)** - Healing and objectives
- ✅ **Pilot Perks** - Player customization
- ✅ **4 rounds** - Game length

### What We Removed:
- ❌ **Pre-game calculations** - Color synergy
- ❌ **Mid-game modifications** - Equipment Cubes
- ❌ **Edge case rules** - Veteran option
- ❌ **Unnecessary markers** - AT tokens
- ❌ **Restrictive rules** - Board edge spacing

### Result:
**Faster setup, cleaner gameplay, easier to teach, same tactical depth.**

## 📋 Updated Quick Reference

### Game Setup (5 Phases):
1. **Terrain** - Place pieces 15cm apart (buildings can stack)
2. **Build** - 10 minutes, 1 Core + 1 Top, any colors
3. **Scenario** - Place Objectives and Resource Cubes (Neuralloy)
4. **Deploy** - Flip coin, loser picks zone first
5. **Combat** - 4 rounds, 2 AP + free REPOSITION per turn

### Actions (2 AP per turn):
- **MOVE (1 AP)** - Pure Legs 15cm+climb, Pure Wheels/Treads 30cm, Mixed 15cm
- **ENGAGE (1 AP)** - Fire weapon, can fire 2 different weapons per turn
- **SCAVENGE (1 AP)** - Pick up HP tokens or Resource Cubes
- **REPOSITION (0 AP)** - Free, once per turn, rotate/adjust pose

### Markers Needed:
- **HP Markers** - Lost health (place at feet when damaged)
- **Resource Cubes** - Neuralloy (+1 HP when scavenged)
- **Objective Markers** - Scenario goals
- **Hazard Cubes** - Dangerous terrain (optional)

### Perks:
- Choose 1 Perk before game starts
- No color restrictions
- Use once per match, then flip card
