# Frame System Design - Multiple Mechs vs Giant Mechs

## 🎯 Core Concept

Players own **FRAMES** (Cores). Each frame can be built as a separate mech or combined into a larger mech. This creates strategic choices:
- **Multiple Small Mechs:** More activations, tactical flexibility, redundancy
- **Single Giant Mech:** More HP, more weapons, intimidating presence

---

## ⚙️ Frame Tiers (Game Sizes)

### 🔹 SKIRMISH (3 Frames per player)
**Build Options:**
- 3 Solo Mechs (3 activations)
- 1 Solo + 1 Duo (2 activations)
- 1 Trio Mech (1 activation)

**Recommended For:** Quick games, learning, 30-45 minutes

### 🔸 BATTLE (5 Frames per player)
**Build Options:**
- 5 Solo Mechs (5 activations)
- 3 Solo + 1 Duo (4 activations)
- 1 Solo + 2 Duo (3 activations)
- 1 Solo + 1 Quad (2 activations)
- 1 Penta Mech (1 activation)

**Recommended For:** Standard games, 60-90 minutes

### 🔶 WAR (7 Frames per player)
**Build Options:**
- 7 Solo Mechs (7 activations)
- Various combinations
- 1 Hepta Mech (1 activation)

**Recommended For:** Epic games, 90-120 minutes

---

## 📐 Frame Combination Rules

### Solo Mech (1 Frame)
- **Base HP:** 4
- **Max Equipment:** 8 slots
- **Activations:** 1 per mech
- **Build Time:** 10 minutes total

### Duo Mech (2 Frames)
- **Base HP:** 8 (4 per frame)
- **Max Equipment:** 16 slots
- **Activations:** 1 (controls both frames as one unit)
- **Build Time:** 10 minutes total
- **Special:** Can split into 2 Solo mechs if both frames survive

### Trio Mech (3 Frames)
- **Base HP:** 12 (4 per frame)
- **Max Equipment:** 24 slots
- **Activations:** 1 (controls all frames as one unit)
- **Build Time:** 10 minutes total
- **Special:** Can split into smaller mechs if frames survive

### Quad/Penta/Hepta Mechs
- **Base HP:** 4 per frame
- **Max Equipment:** 8 slots per frame
- **Activations:** 1 (controls all frames as one unit)
- **Build Time:** 10 minutes total

---

## 🎮 Gameplay Mechanics

### Activation Advantage
**Multiple Small Mechs = More Activations**

Example (3 Frames):
- **3 Solo Mechs:** You activate 3 times per round (alternating with opponent)
- **1 Trio Mech:** You activate 1 time per round (opponent activates 3 times)

**Tactical Implication:** Multiple mechs can react to enemy movements, surround targets, and control more board space.

### HP Pool Advantage
**Giant Mechs = Larger HP Pool**

Example (3 Frames):
- **3 Solo Mechs:** 4 HP each = 12 HP total, but losing 1 mech = lose 1/3 of your force
- **1 Trio Mech:** 12 HP total, but can absorb damage across all frames before losing combat effectiveness

**Tactical Implication:** Giant mechs are harder to kill but losing them is catastrophic.

### Equipment Density
**Giant Mechs = More Weapons on One Platform**

Example (3 Frames):
- **3 Solo Mechs:** 8 weapons max per mech, spread across 3 locations
- **1 Trio Mech:** 24 weapons max on one platform = devastating alpha strike

**Tactical Implication:** Giant mechs can focus fire, but small mechs can flank.

---

## 🔧 Frame Splitting Mechanic

### During Build Phase:
Players declare which frames are combined into which mechs.

### During Game:
**EMERGENCY SPLIT (2 AP):** A multi-frame mech can split into smaller mechs if:
1. All frames are still alive (HP > 0)
2. You spend 2 AP to perform the split
3. Each resulting mech must be able to stand on its own

**Example:**
- Duo Mech (8 HP) takes 3 damage → 5 HP remaining
- Spend 2 AP to split into 2 Solo Mechs
- Frame 1: 3 HP, Frame 2: 2 HP (distribute remaining HP)
- Now you have 2 separate mechs that activate independently

**Tactical Use:** Split when surrounded, when you need more activations, or to escape.

---

## 💀 Frame Destruction

### Solo Mech:
- Reaches 0 HP → Destroyed
- Remove from game

### Multi-Frame Mech:
- **Frame Destruction:** When a multi-frame mech loses HP equal to one frame's worth (4 HP), one frame is destroyed
- **Choose Which Frame:** The owner chooses which frame is destroyed
- **Remaining Frames:** Continue fighting as a smaller mech

**Example:**
- Trio Mech (12 HP) takes 5 damage → 7 HP remaining
- Owner declares Frame 1 is destroyed (4 HP lost)
- Remaining: Duo Mech with 7 HP (3 HP remaining on Frame 2, 4 HP on Frame 3)

**Tactical Implication:** Giant mechs degrade gracefully but lose equipment as frames are destroyed.

## 🎯 Strategic Considerations

### Why Build Multiple Small Mechs?

✅ **More Activations:** React to enemy movements, control board space
✅ **Tactical Flexibility:** Flank, surround, split forces
✅ **Redundancy:** Losing one mech doesn't cripple your army
✅ **Objective Control:** Cover more objectives simultaneously
✅ **Harder to Focus Fire:** Enemy must split attention

❌ **Lower HP per Unit:** Each mech dies faster
❌ **Less Equipment Density:** Fewer weapons per platform
❌ **Easier to Isolate:** Individual mechs can be picked off

### Why Build One Giant Mech?

✅ **Massive HP Pool:** 12+ HP is hard to kill
✅ **Equipment Density:** 24+ weapons = devastating firepower
✅ **Intimidation:** Psychological advantage
✅ **Simplified Activation:** Only need to activate once
✅ **Concentrated Force:** All firepower in one location

❌ **Fewer Activations:** Opponent activates more often
❌ **Single Point of Failure:** Losing it = game over
❌ **Less Board Control:** Can only be in one place
❌ **Easier to Surround:** Multiple enemies can flank
❌ **Harder to Build:** 10 minutes to build a 24-slot mech is challenging

## 🎲 Victory Points Adjustment

To balance the activation advantage, adjust VP scoring:

### Frame-Based VP
- **Solo Mech Destroyed:** 1 VP
- **Duo Mech Destroyed:** 2 VP
- **Trio Mech Destroyed:** 3 VP
- **Quad+ Mech Destroyed:** 4+ VP

**Rationale:** Destroying a giant mech should be worth more VP since it's harder to kill.

### Alternative: HP-Based VP
- **1 VP per 4 HP destroyed** (1 VP per frame destroyed)
- Simpler, scales automatically

---

## 🔄 Frame Reconfiguration (Optional Advanced Rule)

**Between Rounds:** Players can spend 1 full round (skip all activations) to reconfigure their frames:
- Split a multi-frame mech into smaller mechs
- Combine solo mechs into a larger mech
- Rebuild equipment loadouts

**Tactical Use:** Adapt to changing battlefield conditions.

---

## 🏗️ Build Phase Changes

### Current: 10 minutes to build 1 mech
### New: 10 minutes to build ALL mechs

**Time Pressure:** Building multiple mechs in 10 minutes is harder than building one giant mech.

**Skill Factor:** Experienced players can build 3 mechs quickly, new players might struggle.

**Balance:** This naturally balances the activation advantage — multiple mechs are harder to build.

---

## 📝 Rules Summary

### Setup
1. Choose game tier (Skirmish/Battle/War)
2. Each player gets X frames (Cores)
3. Decide how to combine frames (Solo/Duo/Trio/etc.)
4. Build all mechs in 10 minutes

### During Game
- **Activations:** Each mech activates once per round
- **HP Tracking:** Track HP per frame (4 HP each)
- **Frame Destruction:** When a frame reaches 0 HP, it's destroyed
- **Emergency Split:** Spend 2 AP to split a multi-frame mech

### Victory
- **VP per Frame Destroyed:** 1 VP per frame (or use scenario-specific VP)
- **Most VP after 4 Rounds wins**

## 🎨 Thematic Integration

### Lore Justification
**Modular Apex-Frames:** The EDC's modular design philosophy extends to frame configuration. Pilots can:
- **Solo Configuration:** Maximum agility and tactical flexibility
- **Combined Configuration:** Link multiple frames via neural interface for overwhelming firepower

**The Signal Risk:** Larger mechs require more complex neural interfaces, increasing the risk of Signal corruption. Solo mechs are safer but less powerful.

## 🧪 Playtesting Recommendations

### Test Scenarios:
1. **3v3 Solo Mechs vs 1 Trio Mech** (Skirmish)
   - Does the activation advantage balance the HP pool?
   
2. **5 Solo Mechs vs 1 Penta Mech** (Battle)
   - Can the giant mech survive being surrounded?
   
3. **Mixed Forces** (Battle)
   - 1 Solo + 2 Duo vs 3 Solo + 1 Duo
   - Does variety create interesting gameplay?

### Balance Metrics:
- **Win Rate:** Should be ~50/50 between strategies
- **Fun Factor:** Both strategies should feel viable and fun
- **Build Time:** Can players build multiple mechs in 10 minutes?
- **Activation Balance:** Does the activation advantage feel fair?
