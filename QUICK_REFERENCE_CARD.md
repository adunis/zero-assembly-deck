# 🎲 ZERO ASSEMBLY - QUICK REFERENCE

## COMBAT PROCEDURE

### 1. ENGAGE Action (1 AP)
1. **Choose weapon** on your mech
2. **Declare target** (enemy mech)
3. **Check line of sight** (can you see any part of target?)
4. **Measure range** (Short/Medium/Long)
5. **Roll 2D6** + modifiers
6. **Need 7+ to hit** (before modifiers)
7. **Deal damage** if hit (weapon's DP value)

---

## TO-HIT MODIFIERS

### Range
- **Short (0-15cm):** +1 to hit
- **Medium (15-30cm):** 0 to hit
- **Long (30-45cm):** -1 to hit
- **Beyond 45cm:** Cannot shoot

### Cover
- **No Cover:** 0
- **Partial Cover (25-75% obscured):** -1 to hit
- **Heavy Cover (75%+ obscured):** -2 to hit

### Movement
- **Target moved last turn:** -1 to hit
- **Shooter moved this turn:** -1 to hit

## ACTIONS (2 AP per turn)

### MOVE (1 AP)
- **Pure Legs:** 15cm, can climb up to 5cm
- **Pure Wheels/Treads:** 30cm, cannot climb
- **Mixed:** 15cm, cannot climb

### ENGAGE (1 AP)
- Fire weapon at enemy in LoS
- Roll 2D6, need 7+ to hit
- Can ENGAGE twice with different weapons

### SCAVENGE (1 AP)
- Pick up HP tokens or Resource Cubes touching your mech
- HP tokens restore health
- Resource Cubes grant +1 HP

### REPOSITION (0 AP, Free)
- Rotate, bend joints, adjust pose, stand up
- Cannot change position on table
- Once per turn

---

## SPECIAL WEAPON RULES

### Melee Weapons
- **Range:** Base contact only
- **To Hit:** Automatic (no roll)
- **Example:** Bunker Pile Driver (3 DP)

### Ignore Partial Cover
- Shotguns and heavy cannons
- Still affected by Heavy Cover
- **Example:** M10 Breacher Shotgun (2 DP)

### Ignore All Cover
- Artillery weapons (missiles)
- Can shoot over terrain
- **Example:** Starstreak Missile Rack (1 DP × 3 shots)

### Multi-Shot Weapons
- Roll separately for each shot
- Each shot needs 7+ to hit
- **Example:** Twin-Link Assault Rifle (1 DP × 2 shots)

### Knockback Weapons
- Push target on hit
- Can cause fall damage
- **Example:** Sonic Repulsor (0 DP, push 15cm)

### Hazard Weapons
- Place markers on battlefield
- Enemies touching markers take damage
- **Example:** Skorpion Mine Dispenser (2 markers, 1 DP each)

---

## DAMAGE & HP

### Taking Damage
1. When hit, place HP tokens at your mech's feet
2. Number of tokens = weapon's DP value
3. Tokens are no longer part of your HP pool
4. Anyone can scavenge them

### Healing
- **SCAVENGE HP tokens:** Pick up tokens touching your mech
- **SCAVENGE Resource Cubes:** Gain +1 HP immediately
- **Maximum HP:** 4 HP (unless equipment increases it)

### Destruction
- **0 HP = Destroyed**
- Remove mech from battlefield
- Game ends if your only mech is destroyed

---

## TURN STRUCTURE

### Round Sequence
1. **Player 1** takes turn (2 AP + free REPOSITION)
2. **Player 2** takes turn (2 AP + free REPOSITION)
3. **Round ends**
4. Repeat for 4 rounds total

### Turn Order
- Player who went first in Round 1 goes second in Round 2
- Alternates each round

### Game End
- **After Round 4:** Count Victory Points, most VP wins
- **Core destroyed:** Game ends immediately, survivor wins

---

## LINE OF SIGHT

### Checking LoS
1. Get down to eye level with your mech
2. Draw imaginary line from weapon to target
3. If you can see any part of target = LoS exists
4. If terrain completely blocks view = No LoS

### Cover vs. Blocked LoS
- **Partial obstruction:** Cover modifier (-1 or -2)
- **Complete obstruction:** Cannot shoot at all

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

❌ **Forgetting movement penalty** - If you moved this turn, -1 to hit
❌ **Stacking cover wrong** - Partial + Heavy = Heavy (use worst)
❌ **Measuring from wrong point** - Measure from mech base, not weapon tip
❌ **Forgetting LoS check** - Always check LoS before rolling
❌ **Using same weapon twice** - Can only ENGAGE twice with different weapons

---

## TIPS & TACTICS

### Positioning
✅ Use terrain for cover (-1 or -2 to enemy hits)
✅ Get close for accuracy bonus (+1 at Short range)
✅ Stand still for better shots (avoid -1 penalty)

### Weapon Choice
✅ Melee weapons auto-hit but require base contact
✅ Artillery ignores cover but has -1 at Long range
✅ Multi-shot weapons increase hit probability

### Movement
✅ Pure Wheels/Treads = 30cm (fast but no climbing)
✅ Pure Legs = 15cm + climbing (versatile)
✅ Mixed = 15cm no climbing (avoid this)

### Resource Management
✅ Scavenge your own HP tokens to heal
✅ Scavenge enemy HP tokens to deny healing
✅ Resource Cubes grant +1 HP (can exceed max)
