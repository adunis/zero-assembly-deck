# Revised Game System

## 🎮 Core Rules

### Players Control
- **Maximum 2 Frames per player**
- **2 AP per turn total** (not per mech!)
- Can spend AP on any of your mechs

### Frame Options
- **1 Solo Mech** (1 Frame): 4 HP, 8 equipment slots
- **2 Solo Mechs** (2 Frames): 4 HP each, 8 slots each
- **1 Duo Mech** (2 Frames): 8 HP, 16 equipment slots

---

## 📋 Game Phases

### Phase 1: TERRAIN BUILDING
1. Players alternate placing terrain pieces
2. **Placement Rules:**
   - Must be 15cm from other terrain
   - Must be 15cm from board edges
3. Continue until terrain is complete

### Phase 2: SCAVENGING (Build Phase)
1. Set 10-minute timer
2. Build your mech(s) from available parts
3. **Veteran Option:** Players with existing mechs can:
   - Skip scavenging and use existing mech, OR
   - Participate in scavenging and choose at end (existing vs new)

### Phase 3: DEPLOYMENT
1. Place scenario objectives
2. Flip coin: loser picks zone first, winner deploys last

### Phase 4: COMBAT
1. Play 4 Rounds
2. Alternate activations
3. Each player gets 2 AP per turn (spend on any mech)

---

## ⚡ Action Points (AP)

### 2 AP Per Turn Total
- **NOT per mech!**
- Spend on any of your mechs
- Can split between mechs or use all on one

### Examples:

**Player with 2 Solo Mechs:**
- Option A: Mech 1 MOVE + ENGAGE (2 AP used)
- Option B: Mech 1 MOVE, Mech 2 MOVE (2 AP used)
- Option C: Mech 1 ENGAGE, Mech 2 ENGAGE (2 AP used)

**Player with 1 Duo Mech:**
- MOVE + ENGAGE (2 AP used on one mech)

---

## 🎯 Perks System (Simplified)

### All Perks Are Once Per Match
- **Use:** Activate the perk ability
- **Track:** Flip the card face-down after use
- **Multiple Mechs:** Choose which mech uses the perk

### Perk Types:

1. **Offensive Perks** - Boost attacks
2. **Defensive Perks** - Reduce damage
3. **Mobility Perks** - Extra movement
4. **Tactical Perks** - Special abilities
5. **Multi-Mech Perks** - Synergize with having 2 mechs

---

## 🎲 Strategic Balance

### 1 Solo Mech vs 2 Solo Mechs

**1 Solo Mech (1 Frame):**
- ✅ All equipment on one platform
- ✅ Simpler to manage
- ✅ Easier to build in 10 minutes
- ❌ Single point of failure
- ❌ Can only be in one place

**2 Solo Mechs (2 Frames):**
- ✅ Redundancy (losing one doesn't end game)
- ✅ Board control (two locations)
- ✅ Flanking opportunities
- ❌ Split firepower
- ❌ Harder to build in 10 minutes
- ❌ Must divide 2 AP between them

### 2 Solo Mechs vs 1 Duo Mech

**2 Solo Mechs:**
- ✅ Two separate targets
- ✅ Board control
- ✅ Flexibility
- ❌ 4 HP each (die faster)
- ❌ 8 slots each (less firepower per unit)

**1 Duo Mech:**
- ✅ 8 HP total (harder to kill)
- ✅ 16 equipment slots (more firepower)
- ✅ Concentrated force
- ❌ Single target
- ❌ Can only be in one place

---

## 🎯 Why This Works

### No Activation Advantage
- Everyone gets 2 AP per turn
- No matter how many mechs you have
- Balances multiple mechs vs giant mech

### Meaningful Choice
- **2 Mechs:** Flexibility and redundancy
- **1 Mech:** Concentrated power
- Both strategies viable

### Time Pressure
- Building 2 mechs in 10 minutes is harder
- Natural balance through build difficulty

---

## 🏆 Victory Points

### Frame-Based VP
- **1 VP per Frame destroyed**
- Solo Mech = 1 VP
- Duo Mech = 2 VP

### Scenario-Specific VP
- Objectives, flags, etc.
- See scenario rules

## 📝 Example Turn

**Player A:** 2 Solo Mechs (Mech 1 and Mech 2)
**Player B:** 1 Duo Mech

### Round 1, Player A's Turn:
- **Mech 1:** MOVE (1 AP) - advance toward objective
- **Mech 2:** MOVE (1 AP) - flank enemy
- **Total:** 2 AP used

### Round 1, Player B's Turn:
- **Duo Mech:** MOVE (1 AP) + ENGAGE (1 AP) - advance and shoot
- **Total:** 2 AP used

### Round 2, Player A's Turn:
- **Mech 1:** ENGAGE (1 AP) - shoot at Duo Mech
- **Mech 1:** ENGAGE (1 AP) - shoot again
- **Total:** 2 AP used (both on Mech 1)

### Round 2, Player B's Turn:
- **Duo Mech:** REPOSITION (1 AP) + ENGAGE (1 AP)
- **Total:** 2 AP used

## 🎨 Veteran Mech System

### Bringing Existing Mechs

**Option 1: Use Existing Mech**
- Skip scavenging phase
- Use your pre-built mech
- Advantage: Familiar with build
- Disadvantage: Can't adapt to scenario

**Option 2: Build New + Choose**
- Participate in scavenging
- Build a new mech
- At end, choose: existing or new
- Advantage: Flexibility
- Disadvantage: Time pressure (building while deciding)

### Why This Works
- Rewards veteran players (can use proven builds)
- Encourages experimentation (can try new builds)
- Maintains fairness (everyone has same options)

## 🎯 Multi-Mech Perk Examples

### Coordinated Strike (Once Per Match)
**Effect:** If both your mechs can see the same target, they both gain +1 DP on their next attack this turn.
**Use Case:** Flanking bonus

### Tactical Relay (Once Per Match)
**Effect:** One of your mechs can use an action, then immediately your other mech can use an action, ignoring normal AP costs. (Effectively gives you 2 extra AP this turn)
**Use Case:** Alpha strike or emergency response

### Covering Fire (Once Per Match)
**Effect:** When one of your mechs is hit, your other mech can immediately make a free ENGAGE action against the attacker (if in range).
**Use Case:** Punish enemies for attacking

### Emergency Regroup (Once Per Match)
**Effect:** Both your mechs can MOVE toward each other for 0 AP.
**Use Case:** Reposition quickly

### Synchronized Assault (Once Per Match)
**Effect:** This turn, both your mechs can ENGAGE the same target. If both hit, deal +2 DP total.
**Use Case:** Focus fire

## 📊 Comparison: Old vs New System

### OLD SYSTEM:
- 3-7 frames per player
- 1 activation per mech
- 3 Solo mechs = 3 activations = huge advantage
- Complex tier system

### NEW SYSTEM:
- 2 frames max per player
- 2 AP per turn total (not per mech)
- 2 Solo mechs = same 2 AP as 1 Duo mech = balanced
- Simple and fair

---

## 🎮 Why 2 AP Total Works

### Prevents Activation Spam
- Can't activate both mechs fully each turn
- Must choose: focus fire or spread actions

### Creates Tactical Decisions
- "Do I move both mechs or shoot with one?"
- "Do I focus on one mech or split attention?"
- "Do I use my perk now or save it?"

### Maintains Balance
- 1 mech vs 2 mechs = same AP budget
- Choice is about positioning vs firepower
- Not about action economy

## 📝 Rules Summary

### Setup:
1. Build terrain (15cm spacing)
2. Scavenge/build mechs (10 minutes, max 2 frames)
3. Deploy mechs

### Gameplay:
- 2 AP per turn total
- Spend on any of your mechs
- Perks: once per match, flip card after use

### Victory:
- 1 VP per frame destroyed
- Scenario-specific VP
- Most VP after 4 rounds wins

This system is **simpler, more balanced, and more tactical** than the old frame system!
