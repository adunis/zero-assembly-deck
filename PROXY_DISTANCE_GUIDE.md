# PROXY DISTANCE EXPLAINED

## What is "Proxy Distance"?

**Proxy Distance** = How far you can place the shooting device from your mech when firing.

This is NOT about how far the projectile travels - it's about **tactical positioning** of the device itself.

---

## Why Does This Matter?

Different proxy distances give different tactical advantages:

### 🎯 **SHORT** (1 Short Ruler)
- **Used by:** Most cannons (Small/Big Air Cannons, Spring Cannons)
- **Placement:** Device 1 Short Ruler away from your mech
- **Advantage:** Direct line-of-sight shooting
- **Examples:** Autocannons, Heavy Cannons, Railguns, Energy Weapons

### 🚀 **LONG** (1 Long Ruler)
- **Used by:** Missile Launchers (Mortar Air Cannons)
- **Placement:** Device 1 Long Ruler away from your mech
- **Advantage:** Can shoot around corners! Place device behind cover to simulate curving missiles
- **Examples:** Brimstone Micro-Missiles, Starstreak Missiles, Carpet-Bombing Pod

### 👊 **TOUCH** (Physical Contact)
- **Used by:** Melee weapons
- **Placement:** Must be touching enemy mech
- **Advantage:** Guaranteed hit, massive damage
- **Examples:** Pile Driver (3 DP!)

### 💥 **CLOSE** (Within 10cm)
- **Used by:** Point-blank weapons
- **Placement:** Device within 10cm of target
- **Advantage:** Close-quarters devastating effects
- **Examples:** Breacher Shotgun (disables weapons)

---

## Tactical Examples

### Example 1: Direct Fire (SHORT)
```
Your Mech [weapon pointing →] 
         ↓ (1 Short Ruler)
    [Air Cannon Device] → → → Enemy Mech
```
Standard shooting. Device placed 1 Short Ruler away in weapon's direction.

### Example 2: Indirect Fire (LONG)
```
Your Mech [missile pod ↑]
    |
    | (1 Long Ruler away)
    |
[Mortar Device] ⤴ ⤴ ⤴
                  ↓
              Enemy behind wall
```
You can place the mortar device 1 Long Ruler from your mech, even behind a corner, to simulate missiles curving around obstacles!

### Example 3: Melee (TOUCH)
```
Your Mech [pile driver] → touching → Enemy Mech
```
No device needed. Physical contact = 3 DP!

## Card Display

Equipment cards now show:
- **DAMAGE:** How many DP on hit (red)
- **PROXY:** How far device can be placed (blue)
- **DEVICE:** Which toy to use (gray)

## Quick Reference

| Proxy Distance | Placement Rule | Tactical Use |
|----------------|----------------|--------------|
| **SHORT** | 1 Short Ruler | Direct fire |
| **LONG** | 1 Long Ruler | Indirect fire, shoot around corners |
| **TOUCH** | Physical contact | Melee combat |
| **CLOSE** | Within 10cm | Point-blank special effects |

---

## Why "Proxy"?

The device is a **proxy** (stand-in) for your mech's weapon. The proxy distance determines how much tactical flexibility you have in positioning the shot!
