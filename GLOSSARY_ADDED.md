## GLOSSARY STRUCTURE

### 1. Game Terms
- **AP (Action Points)** - Currency for actions (2 per turn)
- **DP (Damage Points)** - Weapon damage amount
- **HP (Hit Points)** - Health pool (Core = 4 HP)
- **SP (Shield Points)** - Energy shield capacity
- **LoS (Line of Sight)** - Required for Direct Fire
- **VP (Victory Points)** - Score for objectives

### 2. Mech Components
- **Core** - Central chassis (4 HP, required)
- **Top** - Seals the Core (required)
- **Legs** - 15cm movement + climbing
- **Wheels/Treads** - 30cm movement, no climbing
- **Weapons** - Equipment that deals damage
- **Armor** - Adds HP or blocks damage
- **Shields** - Energy barriers with SP

### 3. Actions
- **MOVE (1 AP)** - Advance one ruler, push terrain, ram enemies
- **ENGAGE (1 AP)** - Fire weapon, roll 2D6 (need 7+)
- **SCAVENGE (1 AP)** - Pick up HP tokens or Resource Cubes
- **RECHARGE (1 AP)** - Restore 1 SP to shield
- **REPOSITION (0 AP)** - Free action, rotate/stand up

### 4. Weapon Types
- **Direct Fire** - Requires LoS, affected by cover
- **Indirect Fire** - No LoS, ignores cover, -1 to hit
- **Lock-On** - 2-turn process, auto-hit, min range >15cm
- **Melee** - Base contact, auto-hit

### 5. Ranges
- **Short** - 0-15cm (+1 to hit)
- **Medium** - 15-30cm (no modifier)
- **Long** - 30-45cm (-1 to hit)
- **Melee** - Base contact (auto-hit)

### 6. Cover
- **No Cover** - No modifier
- **Partial Cover** - 25-75% hidden (-1 to hit)
- **Heavy Cover** - 75%+ hidden (-2 to hit)
- **Note:** Indirect Fire and Lock-On ignore cover

### 7. Finger Flick
- **When:** After successful hit
- **How Many:** 1-2 DP = 1 Flick | 3 DP = 2 Flicks
- **Effects:** Slides back, falls over, ledge falls (+1 DP)
- **Blocked By:** Active energy shields

### 8. Battlefield Objects
- **HP Tokens** - Lost health, can be scavenged
- **Resource Cubes** - Neuralloy salvage (+1 HP)
- **Objective Markers** - Scenario goals (VP)
- **Hazard Markers** - Dangerous terrain (1 DP)
- **LOCK Tokens** - Mark locked targets

### 9. Dice Rolls
- **To-Hit Roll:** 2D6, need 7+ (before modifiers)
- **Modifiers:** Range, cover, movement
- **Lock Roll:** 2D6, need 7+ to establish lock
- **Countermeasure Roll:** 1D6, varies by equipment

### 10. Terrain
- **Climbing** - Pure Legs climb up to 5cm
- **Pushing** - Push terrain during movement
- **Ramming** - Ram enemies (only touch your mech)
- **Falling** - Falls >5cm = 1 DP
- **Collapse** - Terrain falls on mech = 1 DP

### 11. Keywords
- **Multi-Shot X** - Roll X times
- **Heavy** - 2 Flicks instead of 1
- **Knockback X** - X Flicks on hit
- **Piercing** - Ignores Partial Cover
- **Hazard X** - Place X hazard markers
- **Reliable** - Re-roll one miss per turn
- **Reactive** - Auto-trigger, no AP cost
- **Limited X** - X uses per game
- **Unlimited** - No usage limit

### 12. Game Structure
- **Round** - Both players take one turn (game = 4 rounds)
- **Turn** - One player spends 2 AP + 1 free REPOSITION
- **Phase** - Setup stages (Terrain → Build → Scenario → Deploy → Combat)
- **Turn Order** - Alternates each round

### 13. Lore Terms
- **The Signal** - AI corruption (90 seconds)
- **Apex-Frame** - Modular war machine (10-minute build)
- **Neuralloy** - Rare quantum alloy (Resource Cubes)
- **Tactical Doctrine** - Human pilots only, no AI
- **Factions** - EDC, Collective, EPS, AIU
