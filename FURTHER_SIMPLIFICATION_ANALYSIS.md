# FURTHER SIMPLIFICATION ANALYSIS
## Zero Assembly Rules Review

After reviewing all current rules, here are potential simplifications to make the game even more accessible:

---

## 🔴 HIGH PRIORITY SIMPLIFICATIONS

### 1. **Remove Veteran Option (Phase 2)**
**Current:** Players can keep mechs from previous games and skip the build phase
**Issue:** Edge case that adds complexity for minimal benefit
**Simplification:** Remove entirely. Every game starts with a fresh 10-minute build
**Impact:** Removes 1 paragraph from phase_2, simplifies tournament play

### 2. **Simplify Terrain Spacing Rules (Phase 1)**
**Current:** "15cm from other terrain" AND "15cm from board edges" with building exceptions
**Issue:** Two spacing rules to remember
**Simplification:** Just "15cm from other terrain" - remove board edge rule
**Impact:** Easier to remember, faster terrain placement

### 3. **Remove Building Collapse Damage (Phase 1)**
**Current:** Falling buildings deal 1 DP to mechs they touch
**Issue:** Rare edge case that requires tracking physics interactions
**Simplification:** Buildings can fall over (physics), but don't deal damage
**Impact:** Removes damage tracking for terrain, keeps physics fun without bookkeeping

### 4. **Simplify Deployment (Phase 4)**
**Current:** Coin flip → loser picks zone first → winner deploys last
**Issue:** Two-step process with reversal logic
**Simplification:** Coin flip → winner picks their zone and deploys first
**Impact:** Simpler, more intuitive

---

## 🟡 MEDIUM PRIORITY SIMPLIFICATIONS

### 5. **Remove "8 Equipment Slots" Limit**
**Current:** Each Core has 8 equipment slots
**Issue:** Requires counting during 10-minute build
**Simplification:** No slot limit - attach whatever fits physically in 10 minutes
**Impact:** Removes counting, relies on time pressure and physics

### 6. **Simplify Movement Measurement**
**Current:** "Always measure horizontally along the ground. Never diagonally through the air."
**Issue:** Adds a measurement rule that's rarely violated
**Simplification:** Just state the ruler distances, trust players to measure reasonably
**Impact:** Removes a "don't do this" rule

### 7. **Remove Stability Check 30-Second Fix Window**
**Current:** If mech falls during stability check, 30 seconds to fix it
**Issue:** Requires timing a second timer
**Simplification:** If mech falls after 10 minutes, it's illegal - rebuild it
**Impact:** Simpler pass/fail test

### 8. **Consolidate HP Token Rules**
**Current:** HP tokens mentioned in multiple places with different contexts
**Issue:** Scattered information
**Simplification:** One clear section: "When hit, place HP tokens at feet. Anyone can scavenge them."
**Impact:** Clearer, less repetition

---

## 🟢 LOW PRIORITY SIMPLIFICATIONS

### 9. **Remove "Measure Proxy Distance" Language**
**Current:** "Place the shooting device 1 Short Ruler from your mech (proxy distance)"
**Issue:** "Proxy distance" is jargon
**Simplification:** Just say "Place device 1 ruler away from your mech"
**Impact:** Removes technical term

### 10. **Simplify Round Structure**
**Current:** "Play 4 rounds, alternating turns"
**Issue:** Could be clearer
**Simplification:** "Play 4 rounds. Players alternate taking turns until 4 rounds complete."
**Impact:** Slightly clearer

### 11. **Remove "Scenario Integration" Examples from Neuralloy Lore**
**Current:** Lists 5 scenario examples in lore_6
**Issue:** Duplicates information from scenario chapters
**Simplification:** Keep lore focused on lore, scenarios focused on scenarios
**Impact:** Reduces redundancy
