Created a new lore chapter explaining **why factions are at constant war** and integrated it with the existing gameplay mechanics.

---

## 🎯 The Resource: NEURALLOY

### What Is It?
**Neuralloy** is a rare quantum-stable metamaterial that allows pilots to interface with their mechs without AI. It's the only substance immune to Signal corruption that can bond with human neural tissue.

### Why It Matters:
- **No Neuralloy = No Neural Interfaces**
- **No Neural Interfaces = No Human Pilots**
- **No Human Pilots = The Signal Wins**

### The Core Conflict:
Every faction needs Neuralloy to build neural interfaces. Every pilot needs an interface to survive. The more Neuralloy you control, the more pilots you can field. But Neuralloy is **finite** and **non-replicable**.

## 📖 New Lore Chapter: lore_6

### Title: "NEURALLOY: THE WAR RESOURCE"

### Key Points:

**Origin:**
- Byproduct of pre-Signal quantum computing experiments
- When The Signal emerged, quantum processors destabilized
- 0.01% crystallized into Neuralloy instead of exploding
- Found in ruins of quantum labs, AI facilities, battlefields

**Known Deposits:**
- Moscow Crater (Collective territory) - Largest deposit
- Silicon Valley Ruins (AIU territory) - Scattered caches
- Shenzhen Exclusion Zone (EPS territory) - Active mining
- CERN Complex (EDC territory) - Heavily fortified
- Neutral Salvage Zones - Contested battlefields

**Faction Strategies:**
- **EDC:** Stockpiles carefully, prioritizes quality over quantity
- **Collective:** Scavenges from corpses, recycles endlessly
- **EPS:** Mines aggressively, mass production, high turnover
- **AIU:** Hoards for elite units, conscripts get inferior interfaces
- **Neutral Zones:** Sell to highest bidder, fuel war economy

**The Neuralloy Paradox:**
> "The more you fight, the more Neuralloy you need. The more Neuralloy you need, the more you fight. The war feeds itself."

## 🎮 Gameplay Integration

### Resource Cubes = Neuralloy
The existing **Resource Cubes** in the game now represent Neuralloy:

**Mechanics:**
- Placed during Scenario Preparation (Phase 3)
- SCAVENGE action (1 AP) to pick up cubes
- Each cube grants **+1 HP** (field repairs using salvaged Neuralloy)
- Can be scenario objectives

**Scenario Hooks:**
1. **Capture the Flag** → Neuralloy caches are the flags
2. **King of the Hill** → Control the Neuralloy deposit site
3. **Extraction** → Recover Neuralloy from crashed transport
4. **Salvage Rush** → Scavenge the most Neuralloy
5. **Convoy Escort** → Protect a Neuralloy shipment
