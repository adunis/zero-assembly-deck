## National Specializations

### 🇩🇪 Germany - Industrial Powerhouse
**Theme:** Heavy armor, reliable kinetics, industrial melee
**Real-world parallel:** Rheinmetall, H&K, Krupp are actual German defense manufacturers
**Lore:** Germany's industrial capacity makes them the backbone of EDC armor and heavy weapons production

### 🇫🇷 France - Advanced Technology
**Theme:** Energy weapons, missiles, shields, EMP
**Real-world parallel:** Thales, MBDA, Nexter are real French defense companies
**Lore:** French R&D leads in energy shield technology and electronic warfare

### 🇸🇪 Sweden - Mobility & Sensors
**Theme:** Jump jets, actuators, sensors, knockback
**Real-world parallel:** Saab, Bofors, Ericsson are Swedish companies
**Lore:** Swedish engineering excels in mobility systems and targeting technology

### 🇬🇧 United Kingdom - Precision Systems
**Theme:** Railguns, demolition, stabilizers, engines
**Real-world parallel:** BAE Systems, Rolls-Royce are British
**Lore:** British precision engineering produces high-tech weapons and propulsion

### 🇮🇹 Italy - Close Combat & Structure
**Theme:** Shotguns, gatling arrays, structural joints
**Real-world parallel:** Benelli, OTO Melara are Italian
**Lore:** Italian design focuses on close-quarters effectiveness and modular flexibility

### 🇧🇪 Belgium - Reliable Weapons
**Theme:** Dependable kinetic weapons
**Real-world parallel:** FN Herstal is Belgian
**Lore:** Belgian firearms are known for reliability in harsh conditions

### 🇨🇭 Switzerland - Precision Engineering
**Theme:** Dual-fire systems, precision
**Real-world parallel:** Oerlikon is Swiss
**Lore:** Swiss precision engineering produces advanced fire control systems

### 🇵🇱 Poland - Structural Engineering
**Theme:** Modular components
**Lore:** Polish manufacturing contributes structural components to the EDC supply chain

Each equipment card now displays:

The flag appears before the equipment name, making country of origin immediately visible.
